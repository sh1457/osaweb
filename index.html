History

The Old Students Association is a Registered Society
under Karnataka State Societies Registration Act as of June 2006.
It is one of the first Alumni organisations formed in Bangalore
and has inspired other such organisations.

Since it’s inception it has been working towards it’s
dream of connecting the Alumni with it’s Alma Mater.

So far we have organised reunions that bring back hundreds of Alumni back to school.
In the most recent reunion we have witnessed the homecoming of a member from the 1989 batch.
We have an annual school quiz “Excelsior”.

We have plans to build upon this foundation and
create something new and provide Alumni interaction services.

Mission

To attract and hold the interest of the Alumni by offering services that stimulate interaction, maintain support mechanisms for Alumni networking and add value to the lives of Alumni while strengthening bonds with the institution that results in a life-long relationship.

Vision

We wish to facilitate the continued interaction of the Alumni with the institution in the interest of sustaining the institution's ability to develop and maintain a commitment to excellence and deliver on it's eminence.
